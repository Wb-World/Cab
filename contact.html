About Us

Welcome to Sri Sakthi Cabs, your trusted partner in safe, reliable, and timely transportation services. Whether you're traveling within the city, heading to the airport, or planning an outstation trip, we offer well-maintained vehicles and courteous drivers to ensure your journey is smooth and stress-free.

With 24/7 availability and a simple booking process, we cater to both individual and corporate travel needs. Our fleet includes hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, and tempo travellers, giving you the flexibility to choose a ride that best suits your comfort and group size.

At Sri Sakthi Cabs, we prioritize punctuality, cleanliness, and customer satisfaction. From short rides to long-distance tours, experience the ease of travel like never before — affordable fares, professional service, and peace of mind guaranteed.
